Create a Community Where You Are

Learn to Host

You want to start a community - we want to help. Start by hosting a weekend service. Open up your home, make some coffee, and invite people you know. If you want to take hosting to the next level and build community, there is a group just for you! When you join, you'll get weekly updates, tips to prepare for service, and connection to a community of hosts all over the country.

Learn about Hosting

Be a Leader

Got hosting down? Time to lead. Leaders are interested in going beyond the weekend–they want to actively follow God and help others do the same. Lead a group that watches service together, serves together, and does life together. As a leader, you can list your group online and get access to all kinds of resources to make your group awesome. (Oh, and we can connect you with a coach too - if you want.)

Apply to Lead a Group

Kids are welcome

Not quite sure what to do with your kids during service? Want them to have a meaningful experience with God? We've got you covered. With Kids’ Club Anywhere, you can download lessons, presentations, and get tips on creating an great environment to help your kids connect with God.
