Create a Community Where You Are

Take the next step and open up your home for the weekend service. You can do this by becoming a host. This is the place to start your community and get the resources to make it awesome.

Tips for Hosting

You gather the people, we'll bring the awesome. Here are the basics of hosting a killer weekend service.

  • Don't sweat the details. People want to hang out with you and the service will be great.
  • Pick a space. Like your living room. Somewhere people can gather, talk, and hang out.
  • Crank up the volume. It's way easier to sing along when your friends can't hear you.
  • Oh, and brew some coffee. It's Crossroads, after all.
